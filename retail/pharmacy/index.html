Overview of Pharmacy Management System

  • What is Pharmacy software?
    Pharmacy software is designed to manage retail pharmacies with medicine expiry management, pharmacy billing, inventory management, accounting activities.
  • Why do pharmacies need Pharmacy POS software?
    Gone are days when pharmacies required only a data entry portal to print bills and measure the sales. In the world of personalization and growing competition, pharmacies need a stable pharmacy management software that can give them the edge

    - to retain and gain more customers without losing margin,

    - stock right with accurate tracking of expiry,

    - meet the statutory and pharmaceutical guidelines, all in a single platform.

    - easy medical billing and accounting

  • What are the key features of the pharmacy management system?

    - Retail Pharmacy Billing: Ease of billing with rack and shelf management, selling right products based on industry regulations

    - Pharmacy Inventory management: Accurate inventory management with expiry control and physical stock auditing

    - Purchasing: Ability to purchase the right products at right time without losing margin

    - Expiry management: Ability to track the medicine expiry

    - Customer Management: Get repeated footfalls with reminders to customers and loyalty management

    - Pharmacy Accounting: One platform to manage entire accounting needs with in-built GST accounting and e-filing

    - Reports: Actional insights with real-time reporting capabilities

  • What are the advantages of using Retail Pharmacy management software?
    There are various benefits of using Pharmacy management software.

    - Operational excellence with end to end procurement management

    - Hassle-free compliance to statutory and industry norms

    - Increased sales and consistent business growth with proper rack and shelf management

    - Track 100% and block drugs with shorter expiry during inward and sales

    - Monitor non-moving, ageing, margins, and short-expiry
